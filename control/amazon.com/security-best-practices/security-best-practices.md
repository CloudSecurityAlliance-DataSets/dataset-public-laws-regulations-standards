## Cybersecurity Awareness Training

Help employees in your organization learn how to identify cybersecurity risks,
including phishing, social engineering, and data privacy with this free,
15-minute training from Amazon. The training is offered in 11 languages, is
compliant with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 AA, and
includes completion certificates for compliance tracking. Deploy immediately
from our website or implement our pre-packaged files in your organization’s
LMS.

[Learn more](https://learnsecurity.amazon.com/)
