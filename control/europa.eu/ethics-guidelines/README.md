# Ethics Guidelines for Trustworthy AI

Ethics Guidelines for Trustworthy AI — defines what makes AI systems trustworthy: lawful (complying with laws), ethical (respecting ethical principles), and robust (technically and socially robust). Predates and informed the EU AI Act.
