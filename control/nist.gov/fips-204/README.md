# Module-Lattice-Based Digital Signature Standard

FIPS 204 (2024) standardizes ML-DSA (Module-Lattice-Based Digital Signature Algorithm, based on CRYSTALS-Dilithium) — NIST's primary post-quantum digital-signature standard.

- **Acronym:** FIPS 204
- **Owner:** National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
- **SecID:** `secid:control/nist.gov/fips-204`
- **Source:** https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/fips/204/final
