# Advanced Encryption Standard (AES)

FIPS 197 (2001, updated 2023) standardizes the Advanced Encryption Standard — the universally-used symmetric block cipher (AES-128, AES-192, AES-256). Foundational cryptographic primitive.

- **Acronym:** FIPS 197
- **Owner:** National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
- **SecID:** `secid:control/nist.gov/fips-197`
- **Source:** https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/fips/197/final
