# Protecting Controlled Unclassified Information in Nonfederal Systems and Organizations

NIST SP 800-171 establishes the security requirements for protecting Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) when residing in nonfederal systems and organizations. Cited by DFARS 252.204-7012 for defense contractors and increasingly by other federal procurement clauses.

- **Acronym:** SP 800-171
- **Owner:** National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
- **SecID:** `secid:control/nist.gov/800-171`
- **Source:** https://csrc.nist.gov/pubs/sp/800/171/r3/final
