# Act on Prevention of Damage Caused by Unauthorized Acts Against Important Computers

Act No. 42 of Reiwa 7 (2025), Japan's first "active cyber defense" law. Authorizes government monitoring/analysis of communications data for threat detection, mandatory incident reporting by critical-infrastructure operators, public-private information-sharing agreements, and government counter-access/neutralization of attacker infrastructure, overseen by a new Cyber Communications Information Management Committee.
