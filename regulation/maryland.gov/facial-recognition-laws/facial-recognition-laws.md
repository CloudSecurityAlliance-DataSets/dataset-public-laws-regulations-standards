# Maryland Facial Recognition Laws

_This is a multi-bill collection. Maryland enacts facial-recognition statutes incrementally rather than as a single named law. Below is HB 338 (2024) — Criminal Procedure / Facial Recognition Technology — Requirements, Procedures, and Prohibitions, as a representative example._

_Source: https://mgaleg.maryland.gov/2024RS/bills/hb/hb0338F.PDF (enrolled bill) — extracted via pdftotext -layout._
