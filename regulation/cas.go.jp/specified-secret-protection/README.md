# Act on the Protection of Specially Designated Secrets

Act No. 108 of 2013, effective December 2014. Organizes national-security secrets into 4 categories (Defense, Diplomacy, Prevention of Specified Harmful Activities, Prevention of Terrorism), governs designation, handler security-clearance vetting, and penalties.
