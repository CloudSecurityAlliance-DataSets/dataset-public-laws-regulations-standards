# ePrivacy Directive

EU ePrivacy Directive (Directive 2002/58/EC, as amended) — governs the processing of personal data and protection of privacy in electronic communications, including cookies, traffic data, and direct marketing. Cookie consent rules derive from this directive.

- **Acronym:** ePrivacy
- **Owner:** European Union
