# Massachusetts AI Legislation Tracker

_This is a tracker page. Massachusetts has many pending AI-related bills (H.83 et al) but no single enacted comprehensive AI law as of acquisition date. Below is a snapshot of the malegislature.gov bill search for 'artificial intelligence' — a list of active and historical AI legislation in MA._

_Source: https://malegislature.gov/Bills/Search?SearchTerms=artificial+intelligence — captured via playwright headless browser._
