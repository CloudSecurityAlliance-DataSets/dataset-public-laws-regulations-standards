# Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act

Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act — federal private-sector privacy law. Replacement law (Bill C-27 CPPA) was proposed but did not pass before 2025 election.

- **Acronym:** PIPEDA
- **Owner:** Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada
- **SecID:** `secid:regulation/canada.ca/pipeda`
- **Source:** https://laws-lois.justice.gc.ca/eng/acts/p-8.6/
