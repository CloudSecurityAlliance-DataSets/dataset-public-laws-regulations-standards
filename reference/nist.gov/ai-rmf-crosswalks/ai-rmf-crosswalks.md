#  Crosswalk Documents

This page lists publicly available crosswalks submitted by the AI RMF user
community. These documents are intended to help other organizations and
individuals use the AI RMF to manage and reduce AI risks. Inclusion of a
crosswalk below does not imply NIST endorsement of the resource the AI RMF is
mapped to, nor does it imply that either resource comprehensively covers the
contents of the other. To be included in this listing, a crosswalk must: map
elements of the AI RMF to a resource that is freely available on the Internet
for others to use without charge; accurately reflect the contents of the AI
RMF in its mappings; and not include or constitute an advertisement for a
product or service. Requests to have a crosswalk listed on this page should be
sent with a brief description and link to [ aiframework@nist.gov
](mailto:aiframework@nist.gov). NIST staff will review all submissions before
posting for adherence with the requirements above.
