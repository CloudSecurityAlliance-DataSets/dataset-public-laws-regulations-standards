# AI Exchange: the world's AI security guide

**300+ pages** of free, constantly-evolving, practical guidance on securing AI
systems. It covers the fundamentals and represents the closest publicly
available alignment of global expert consensus, feeding directly into the AI
Act and ISO standards through a unique SDO partnership.  

The OWASP AI Exchange is a global think tank and open-source initiative that
brings together experts in AI and cybersecurity. Its mission is to bring
clarity in AI security by connecting practitioners, researchers, industry, and
policymakers.

_Technology with global impact requires global action._
